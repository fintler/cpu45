Welcome

The Cedro Peak Ultramarathon is the Albuquerque Road Runner's first foray into hosting an ultramarathon. If the La Luz Trail Run is just too short, you've come to the right place.

CPU45 is a 45 mile and 45 km (28 mile) ultramarathon in the Cibola National Forest. 100% of the proceeds go to charity. The race is an environmentally friendly leave-no-trace event bringing exciting wilderness running to Albuquerque's doorstep.
