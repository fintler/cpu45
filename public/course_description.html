Course Description

The Cedro Peak area is a labyrinth of trails and double track just minutes from Albuquerque, in Tijeras. Starting at the Oak Flat Group Area, the course takes runners through a dense forest of ponderosa pine, juniper, and piñon. It is primarily rolling single track with several steep, but short climbs, and a few short sections of double track and dirt roads. Many sections are very rocky, but there are some fast, non-technical sections.
